How it Works

TalkTravel is a voice chatting application that connects travellers with destination experts that can answer their specific questions through instant conversations. Using it is as simple as it could be.

  • Choose a destination
  • Select communication language
  • Choose topic of interest
  • Get connected to a destination expert
  • Get answers to your questions
  • Leave a quick feedback, if you want
